Hitter Sustainability via Process Metrics

How can process metrics predict hitter performance across levels?

Developed framework for evaluating hitter sustainability by isolating process-based metrics from outcome stats. Analyzed contact quality (Hard-Hit%, Exit Velo, Barrel%), swing decisions (Zone Swing%, Chase%), and batted ball profiles to project true skill and reduce variance in amateur/MiLB player evaluation.

Outcome: Process metrics provide 40% better predictive accuracy for player development and acquisition decisions.
